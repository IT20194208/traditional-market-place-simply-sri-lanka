our category

Art

Explore Sri Lanka's diverse artwork, from traditional paintings capturing cultural themes to contemporary pieces reflecting the evolving art scene. These artworks depict landscapes, traditions, and stories, showcasing the island's beauty and cultural richness.

Craft

Discover Sri Lanka's skilled craftsmanship in woodwork, creating intricate furniture, masks, and ornamental pieces. Appreciate the artistry in brassware, pottery, and vibrant batik textiles, each showcasing the country's cultural heritage through meticulous design and handiwork.

ECO Friendly Items

Discover sustainable treasures encompassing handmade crafts from recycled materials, Ayurvedic wellness products, and eco-friendly packaging solutions. Each item promotes a greener lifestyle, nurturing wellness while minimizing environmental impact, providing conscientious choices for a sustainable and eco-conscious living.

Foods

Curate an authentic Sri Lankan culinary experience with traditional delicacies like kewum, kokis, honey rings, and other flavorful treats. These iconic dishes capture the essence of Sri Lanka's rich culinary heritage, perfect for sharing the country's unique flavors and cultural essence worldwide.

Garments Items

Explore Sri Lanka's textile legacy through saris, sarongs, and handloom textiles. The elegance of vibrant saris, the versatility of sarongs, and the intricate handloom fabrics showcase the country's cultural diversity and craftsmanship, offering timeless attire reflecting Sri Lanka's heritage and style.

House Hold Items

Embrace Sri Lanka's cultural essence within your home through brassware, pottery, woodcraft, and batik textiles. These items exhibit meticulous craftsmanship, cultural motifs, and functional elegance, adding a touch of tradition and artistry to your living spaces, reflecting Sri Lanka's rich heritage and artistic finesse.
